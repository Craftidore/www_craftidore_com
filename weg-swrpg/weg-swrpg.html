West End Games Star Wars RPG

I acquired the reprint of the WEG Star Wars RPG when FFG released it. I really like the system, but I think it has a few flaws. Most eggregious of these flaws are the character templates. These character templates don't feel broad enough, force backstory and personality onto characters, don't provide flexibility of species, and feel quite trope-y. Consequently, I'm making a set of new character templates to replace the existing ones. They are mostly based upon the careers in Fantasy Flight Games.
