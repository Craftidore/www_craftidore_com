Rest assured, it can be made to look better, I'm just not willing to do that on a technical test. This limited kind of website can be hosted for free on Github (which I can help setup). You could keep the website entirely for free if you were willing to have a website address like "https://yourorgname.github.io/", but if you wanted to have it at "https://yourorgname.com" (or even "https://yourorgname.xyz", which would likely be cheaper), you'd need to pay a domain registrar like GoDaddy for it.

I'm willing to take the webpages here, pretty them up a bit to look more like a modern website, look decent on a phone, and put your info in them for free. If, after I've prettied it up, aren't quite happy with how it looks, I'm happy to make modifications to make it look however you like, even doing fancy visual things like image carousels or similar, but at that point I'd start charging $15 an hour.

If you decide you don't think this limited kind of site will fit your long-term needs, and that you'll eventually want to have accounts & members-only pages or payment details handled within the website, don't go with this solution. This solution is good for people that need a website with information on it, like coding documentation, organization information, or blogs, not for sites which need greater interactivity.
